## Overview

The Vincore Delivery Framework (VDF) is the standard project delivery methodology applied to every client engagement regardless of service type, industry, or project size.

Whether building a GTM system, speed-to-lead automation, CRM, AI governance framework, executive workshop, or operations automation — the five phases and 30 documents never change. Only the depth and formality of each document scales with project complexity and budget.

**Small projects ($300–$2,000):** Use the 17 mandatory documents only.
**Mid-size projects ($2,000–$10,000):** Use mandatory + recommended documents (28 total).
**Enterprise projects ($10,000+):** Use all 30 documents.

---

## Document Classification System

### By Requirement
- **Mandatory** — Required on every project regardless of size
- **Recommended** — Applied on most projects; use judgement based on complexity
- **Optional** — Larger or enterprise projects only

### By Audience
- **Client-Facing** — Sent or presented to the client
- **Internal Only** — Vincore operational use only
- **Shared** — Both parties access and approve

## The Benefits Realization Report — Strategic Note

This document is delivered 30–90 days after go-live and is Vincore's most powerful retention and marketing tool. It documents:
- ROI achieved
- Hours saved per week
- Revenue improvements
- Manual work eliminated
- Conversion improvements
- Client outcomes vs original targets

Most automation firms disappear after go-live. Vincore returns with proof. This is what converts one-time clients into retainer clients.

---

## The Marketing Asset Pack — Strategic Note

No project is complete until the Marketing Asset Pack exists. Every completed project automatically feeds the Vincore marketing engine. The case study, metrics, LinkedIn post, and portfolio copy are produced as formal project deliverables — not afterthoughts.
