---

## Pricing Philosophy

1. **Never lead with price.** Understand the problem first. Price based on the value of the problem solved, not hours spent.
2. **Anchor to ROI.** A $500 speed-to-lead system that recovers one lost job per month pays for itself in the first week.
3. **Scope tightly.** Define deliverables clearly in writing before starting. Avoid scope creep.
4. **Deposits are non-negotiable.** 50% upfront for all projects over $300.
5. **Raise prices** after the first 2–3 clients provide testimonials or case study approval.

---

## Project-Based Pricing (Current Stage)

### Starter System — $300–$500
Single-problem automation. One workflow, one use case.

Includes: Discovery call (30 mins), system design and build, testing, deployment, handover documentation, 1 round of revisions.

Examples: Basic speed-to-lead, simple lead capture and notification, single Zapier workflow with Airtable.

### Growth System — $800–$1,500
Multi-step automation covering a core business function.

Includes: Discovery call (60 mins), full system architecture document, build across 3–5 tools, testing, handover call and full documentation, 2 rounds of revisions, 2 weeks post-launch support.

Examples: Full speed-to-lead with scoring and routing, property management invoice and ticket system, real estate lead aggregation and CRM setup.

### Full Operations Build — $2,000–$4,000
End-to-end automation of a business function.

Includes: 2 discovery calls, full process mapping and audit, multi-system architecture, build across 5+ tools, staged testing and QA, staff walkthrough and training, full documentation suite, 30-day post-launch support.
