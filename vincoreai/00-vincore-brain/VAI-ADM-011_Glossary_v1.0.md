# VAI-ADM-011 Glossary of Terms
**Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** Opeyemi Idonuagbe | **Status:** Active

---

## A

**Airtable** — Cloud-based database tool used as CRM, project tracker, and data layer in Vincore automation systems.

**AI Advisory** — Vincore service line covering AI readiness, governance, agent design, and adoption roadmaps.

---

## C

**CAR Methodology** — Challenge, Action, Result. The framework used for all Vincore blog articles and case studies.

**CRM** — Customer Relationship Management. The system used to track leads, clients, and pipeline.

---

## D

**Discovery Call** — A 30-minute free consultation where Vincore maps a prospect's current revenue or operations situation and identifies gaps.

**Done-for-you** — Vincore's delivery model. We build the system. The client receives a working, documented system — not a template or course.

---

## G

**GRC** — Governance, Risk, and Compliance. Opeyemi's professional background that informs Vincore's structured, documented approach to system design.

**GTM** — Go-to-Market. The strategy and systems used to bring a product or service to market and acquire customers.

---

## I

**ICP** — Ideal Customer Profile. A detailed description of the type of business most likely to buy from Vincore and get the most value.

**ISO** — International Organization for Standardization. The framework background that informs Vincore's document control, risk management, and delivery standards.

---

## L

**Lead** — A potential client who has expressed interest or submitted an enquiry.

**Lead Response Time** — The time between a lead submitting an enquiry and receiving a response. Vincore's systems target under 5 minutes.

---

## O

**Operations Systems** — Vincore service line covering workflow automation, onboarding, invoice processing, ticket escalation, and reporting.

---

## R

**Revenue Systems** — Vincore service line covering speed-to-lead, lead scoring, CRM automation, and pipeline visibility.

---

## S

**Speed-to-Lead** — An automated system that ensures inbound leads receive an acknowledgement within minutes and are immediately scored, routed, and logged.

---

## V

**VDF** — Vincore Delivery Framework. The five-phase project delivery methodology: Initiate, Plan, Assess and Design, Execute, Close.

**Vincore Brain** — The 00-vincore-brain folder. The single source of truth for all Vincore AI knowledge.

**Vincore Intelligence Platform** — The long-term vision of interconnected AI agents that handle the full client journey from lead capture to case study creation.

---

## Z

**Zapier** — Automation platform used to connect tools and build workflows. Core tool in Vincore's automation stack.
