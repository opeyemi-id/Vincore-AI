# VAI-ADM-010 Brand Voice and Writing Style Guide
**Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** Opeyemi Idonuagbe | **Status:** Active

---

## Voice Principles

**Lead with the problem, not the service.**
Wrong: "We offer AI automation consulting."
Right: "How fast does your team respond when a lead fills out your contact form?"

**Be specific and concrete.**
Wrong: "We help businesses grow."
Right: "A speed-to-lead system that responds in under 5 minutes makes you significantly more likely to win the job."

**Practitioner tone — not a salesperson, not a professor.**
Write like someone who has built these systems and knows exactly what breaks. No hype. No fluff. Earned confidence.

**Short sentences. Active voice.**
Wrong: "The workflows that have been implemented by our team are designed to..."
Right: "We build workflows that work."

---

## Words We Never Use

synergy, leverage (as a verb), circle back, game-changer, seamless, revolutionary, cutting-edge, world-class, unlock (overused), empower, innovative, disruptive

---

## Punctuation Rules

- No em dashes (—) in client-facing copy. Use a comma, full stop, or restructure the sentence.
- Use the Oxford comma.
- Write numbers under ten as words. Use numerals for 10 and above.
- Spell out "percent" in body copy. Use % in tables and data.

## Article and Content Style

- All articles use the CAR methodology: Challenge, Action, Result
- Author credit always: Opeyemi Idonuagbe, Founder, Vincore AI

**Operations Systems:**
