### Section 1: Executive Summary (Half page)

**Purpose:** Make the client feel immediately understood. Lead with their problem, not your solution.

**Content:**
- One paragraph stating the specific problem the client is experiencing — use their own words from the discovery call where possible
- One paragraph summarising what Vincore will build and the expected outcome
- One line on the investment and timeline

**Tone:** Direct, empathetic, confident. No fluff. No generic consulting language. No em dashes.

**Example opening:**
"[Business Name] is currently managing all client bookings through WhatsApp and Instagram DMs. This means every inquiry requires manual attention, appointments are tracked informally, and no-shows happen with no automated reminder in place. This proposal outlines a fully automated booking and client management system that eliminates manual response work and ensures no booking opportunity is missed."

---

### Section 2: Understanding Your Business (Half page)

**Purpose:** Prove you listened. Mirror back what you learned in the discovery call.

**Content:**
- Business overview (2–3 sentences): what they do, how many staff, how they currently operate
- Current process: how they take bookings, manage clients, follow up, handle payments today
- Key pain points (bullet list of 3–5, specific to this client)
- What success looks like for them

**Do not:** Copy-paste generic industry problems. Every point must be specific to this client.

---

### Section 3: The Proposed Solution

**Purpose:** Show exactly what you will build. No vagueness.

#### 3a. Solution Overview (1 paragraph)

Name the tier being proposed (Website Only / Website + Booking / Full System) and summarise it in plain English.

#### 3b. What's Included

For **Full System** (primary offer):

**Component 1: Branded Website**
- Responsive website (mobile-first)
- Services page with pricing
- About and brand section
- Contact and booking CTA
- Integrated with booking system

**Component 2: Automated Booking System**
- Real-time availability calendar
- Deposit payment via Paystack (Lagos) or Stripe (international)
- Instant booking confirmation sent to client automatically
- Booking logged in CRM automatically

**Component 3: Client Management System (CRM)**
- Airtable-based client database
- Full client history (bookings, payments, referrals, notes)
- Repeat client identification
- Referral tracking

**Component 4: Six Core Automations**
1. Instant booking confirmation (WhatsApp/Email to client)
2. 24-hour appointment reminder (reduces no-shows)
3. Post-appointment follow-up (review request and rebook prompt)
4. Rebooking nudge (sent automatically 3–4 weeks after last visit)
5. Referral thank-you (triggered when a referred client books)
6. Low inventory alert (for product-based services)

**Component 5: Owner Dashboard**
- Real-time view of bookings, revenue, and client activity
- No manual updating required — all data flows automatically

For **Website + Booking** (entry offer):
Include Components 1 and 2 only. Note that Components 3–5 can be added later.

For **Website Only** (fallback offer):
Component 1 only. Note limitations clearly.

#### 3c. What's Not Included

- Staff management or payroll systems
- Accounting software integration (unless specified)
- Social media management or content creation
- Ongoing ad management
- Any platform not listed in the solution above

### Section 4: Why Vincore AI

**Purpose:** Credibility without arrogance.

**Content:**
- 2–3 sentences on Vincore's background (ISO standards, GRC, done-for-you approach)
- Reference to MBH Automation System as proof of concept with 2–3 specific metrics
- One line differentiator: "We do not just build — we document, test, and hand over systems your team can actually use and maintain."

**MBH Reference Stats (update as real data is confirmed):**
- Booking response time reduced from [X hours] to under 2 minutes
- [X] no-shows eliminated through automated reminders
- [X] hours per week saved on manual WhatsApp management

---

### Section 5: The Vincore Delivery Framework

**Purpose:** Show what happens after they say yes. Remove fear of the unknown.

**Format:** Numbered list with brief description of each phase.

1. **Initiate** — We confirm scope, sign the agreement, and collect the deposit. You receive a project timeline within 24 hours.
2. **Plan** — We map your exact workflow and design the system before building anything. You approve the design first.
3. **Build** — We build and configure everything. No technical work required from you.
4. **Test** — We test every scenario. You review the system before it goes live.
5. **Hand Over** — We walk you through the system, provide full documentation, and ensure your team is confident. The system goes live.

**Timeline by tier:**
- Website Only: 5–7 business days
- Website + Booking: 10–14 business days
- Full System: 3–4 weeks

## Agent Instructions

When generating a proposal using this template:
1. Read client discovery notes from their project folder in `09-client-projects/`
2. Pull pricing from `00-vincore-brain/VAI-ADM-004_PricingPhilosophy_v1.0.md`
3. Pull ICP context from `00-vincore-brain/VAI-ADM-003_TargetCustomers_v1.0.md`
4. Fill every bracketed field with specific client information — never leave placeholders
5. Mirror the client's own language from discovery notes wherever possible
6. Default to Full System as the primary recommendation
7. Always include Website + Booking as an alternative
8. Include a Table of Contents after the cover page
9. Save as `CLI-[CODE]-001_Proposal_v1.0.md` in client's Initiate/Commercials folder
10. Update Master Document Register after saving
