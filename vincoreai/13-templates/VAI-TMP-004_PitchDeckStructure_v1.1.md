## Purpose

This document defines the slide-by-slide structure for Vincore AI pitch presentations. Used for prospect meetings, discovery call follow-ups, and speaking opportunities.

The actual .pptx file lives in Google Drive. This markdown version is the agent-readable brief for generating slide content.

---

## Presentation Types

### Type A: Prospect Pitch (Primary)
Used when presenting directly to a business owner after initial contact. 10 slides including TOC. Maximum 15 minutes to present.

### Type B: Discovery Follow-Up
Used after a discovery call to present the proposed solution visually before the written proposal. 8 slides including TOC.

### Type C: Speaking / Event Presentation
Used at industry events, panels, or networking sessions. 12–15 slides including TOC. 20–30 minutes.

## Design Standards (V3 Brand)

**Backgrounds:**
- Primary (dark slides): Ink #081C3A with radial gradient to Abyss #040d1e
- Secondary (light slides): Paper #f2f9ff or White #ffffff

**Text:**
- On dark backgrounds: White #ffffff, Muted rgba(255,255,255,.65)
- On light backgrounds: Body #37415f, Muted #6b7794

**Accent colors:**
- Primary actions/buttons: Electric Blue #2196F3 gradient → #54E0FF
- Highlights/glows: Cyan #54E0FF
- Success/console: Teal #40E0C8
- Warm gradient endpoint: Gold #e3c77e

**Typography:**
- Headings: Space Grotesk 700, letter-spacing −0.02em
- Body: Inter 400, line-height 1.75
- Micro-labels: Inter 700, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.15–0.22em

**Layout rules:**
- Maximum 30 words per slide (excluding tables)
- One idea per slide
- No walls of text
- Vincore AI orb logo bottom right corner on every slide
- Slide numbers bottom left
- Table of Contents always slide 2

**File format:**
- Present as PDF
- Keep .pptx source in Google Drive
- Name: `CLI-[CODE]-002_PitchDeck_v1.0.pdf`

## Agent Instructions

When generating a pitch deck using this template:
1. Identify which Type is needed (A, B, or C)
2. Pull client details from discovery questionnaire in `09-client-projects/`
3. Fill all bracketed fields with real information — no placeholders in final version
4. For metrics: pull from MBH case study in `10-portfolio-case-studies/`
5. Always include Table of Contents as Slide 2
6. Generate slide-by-slide content as structured text
7. Apply V3 brand colors and typography as specified
8. Save as `CLI-[CODE]-002_PitchDeckContent_v1.0.md` in client's Initiate/Commercials folder
9. Update Master Document Register after saving
