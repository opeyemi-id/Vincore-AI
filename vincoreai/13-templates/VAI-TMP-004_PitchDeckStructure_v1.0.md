## Purpose

This document defines the slide-by-slide structure for Vincore AI pitch presentations. Used for prospect meetings, discovery call follow-ups, and speaking opportunities.

The actual .pptx file lives in Google Drive. This markdown version is the agent-readable brief for generating slide content.

---

## Presentation Types

### Type A: Prospect Pitch (Primary)
Used when presenting directly to a business owner after initial contact. 8–10 slides. Maximum 15 minutes to present.

### Type B: Discovery Follow-Up
Used after a discovery call to present the proposed solution visually before the written proposal. 6–8 slides.

### Type C: Speaking / Event Presentation
Used at industry events, panels, or networking sessions. 10–15 slides. 20–30 minutes.

## Type A: Prospect Pitch — Slide by Slide

### Slide 1: Cover

- Vincore AI logo (centered or top left)
- Presentation title: "How [Business Name] Can Stop Losing Bookings and Start Running on Autopilot"
  OR for general use: "From Manual to Automated: What a Revenue System Looks Like for [Industry] Businesses"
- Presenter: Opeyemi Idonuagbe, Founder, Vincore AI
- Date
- vincoreai.com

**Design:** Full navy background. Teal accent on title. White text.

---

### Slide 2: The Problem (Their World)

**Headline:** "You are losing bookings you do not even know about."

**Content:**
3 pain points presented visually (icon + one line each):

1. Every enquiry needs a manual reply — and slow replies lose customers
2. No system tracking who has booked, paid, or needs a reminder
3. The owner is doing the work a system should be doing

**Design:** White background. Navy text. Teal icons.
**No bullet points** — use three icon cards or a simple visual layout.

---

### Slide 3: The Cost of Doing Nothing

**Headline:** "What manual operations actually cost you."

**Content:**
Show 2–3 specific numbers relevant to their industry:

For beauty/salons:
- "Average lash studio loses [X] potential bookings per month to slow DM response"
- "A single no-show at ₦[X] per appointment = ₦[X] lost per month"
- "Business owners spend an average of [X hours] per week on manual booking management"

For HVAC/service businesses:
- "The average business takes 47 hours to follow up on a new lead"
- "The first company to respond wins the job — 78% of the time"
- "One missed emergency call = a lost job worth $[X]"

**Design:** Dark navy background. Big white numbers. Teal for emphasis.

---

## Design Standards

**Colors:**
- Primary background options: Deep Navy (#0F1E3C) or Off-White (#F8F9FA)
- Accent: Electric Teal (#00C9B1)
- Text on dark: White (#FFFFFF)
- Text on light: Near-black (#1A1A2E)

**Fonts:**
- Headings: Space Grotesk Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Accent/labels: Inter SemiBold, uppercase, tracked

**Layout rules:**
- Maximum 30 words per slide (excluding tables and lists)
- One idea per slide
- No walls of text
- Always include the Vincore AI logo in the bottom right corner
- Slide numbers bottom left

**File format:**
- Present as PDF (prevents font issues)
- Keep .pptx source in Google Drive
- Name: `CLI-[CODE]-002_PitchDeck_v1.0.pdf`

5. Generate slide-by-slide content as structured text
6. Flag which slides need design work in the actual .pptx
