## Purpose

This questionnaire is used during or before the discovery call to gather the information needed to write a tailored proposal. It applies to both beauty/salon prospects (Lagos and international) and overseas service business prospects (HVAC, plumbing, etc.).

Complete this for every prospect before writing a proposal. Save completed version in the client's project folder:
`09-client-projects/[Geography]/[Client]/PRJ-001/01-Initiate/Opportunity-Documents/`

---

## Section C: Pain Points (What's Broken)

19. What takes the most time in your day that you wish you didn't have to do manually?

20. Have you ever lost a booking because you responded too slowly? (Yes / No / Not sure)

21. Have you had no-shows that cost you revenue? How often?

22. Is there information about your clients you wish you had but don't currently track?

23. What does a bad week look like operationally — what breaks down?

24. On a scale of 1–10, how organised do you feel your booking and client management currently is?

---

## Section D: Goals and Vision

25. What would "perfectly organised" look like for your business?

26. If you could fix one thing about how you manage bookings and clients, what would it be?

27. Where do you want the business to be in 12 months? (More clients? More locations? Less manual work?)

28. Have you tried any tools or systems before to solve these problems? What happened?

---

## Section E: Commercial Qualification

29. Are you the decision maker for this investment? (Yes / No — if no, who else is involved?)

30. What is your rough monthly revenue? (This helps Vincore size the ROI case correctly)
    - [ ] Under ₦200,000 / $500
    - [ ] ₦200,000–₦500,000 / $500–$1,500
    - [ ] ₦500,000–₦1,000,000 / $1,500–$3,000
    - [ ] Above ₦1,000,000 / $3,000+
    - [ ] Prefer not to say

31. Have you budgeted for this kind of investment? What range are you comfortable with?

32. What is your timeline — how soon would you want this in place?
    - [ ] Immediately
    - [ ] Within 1 month
    - [ ] 1–3 months
    - [ ] Just exploring for now

---

## Section F: Disqualification Check

Answer these internally — do not ask the client directly.

- [ ] Do they have fewer than 5 enquiries/bookings per month? (If yes, automation ROI is too low — flag)
- [ ] Are they unwilling to invest in the deposit? (Flag)
- [ ] Is the decision maker not present on this call? (Flag — do not write a proposal until DM is engaged)
- [ ] Do they want to DIY? (Flag — Vincore is done-for-you only)
