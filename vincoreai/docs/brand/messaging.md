## Voice principles

**Lead with the problem, not the service.**
Wrong: "We offer AI automation consulting."
Right: "How fast does your team respond when a lead fills out your contact form?"

**Be specific and concrete.**
Wrong: "We help businesses grow."
Right: "A speed-to-lead system that responds in under 5 minutes makes you up to 21x more likely to win the job."

**Practitioner tone — not a salesperson, not a professor.**
Write like someone who has built these systems and knows exactly what breaks. No hype. No fluff. Earned confidence.

**Short sentences. Active voice.**
Wrong: "The workflows that have been implemented by our team are designed to..."
Right: "We build workflows that work."

**Never say:** synergy, leverage (as a verb), circle back, game-changer, revolutionary, cutting-edge, world-class, seamless.

## Value proposition by service

### Revenue Systems
*The problem:* Leads coming in but not converting. Slow follow-up. No prioritisation. Wrong person getting notified.
*The promise:* Respond to every lead in under 5 minutes, automatically scored and routed to the right person.

### Operations Systems
*The problem:* Manual tracking, missed handoffs, team drowning in repetitive tasks, no visibility into what's happening.
*The promise:* Automated workflows that route, escalate, and document — so your team focuses on work that matters.

### AI Advisory
*The problem:* Business knows AI matters but doesn't know where to start or what to build.
*The promise:* A clear AI roadmap built on what your business actually needs, not what's trending.

## Differentiator: ISO/GRC background

Vincore's founder has a background in ISO standards, governance, risk, and compliance. This means:
- Systems are documented, not just built
- Risk is considered before deployment, not after
- Handovers include documentation so clients stay in control
- Enterprise-grade rigour applied to SMB problems

**How to use this in copy:**
Don't lead with "ISO certified" — nobody cares about the credential. Lead with what it means: *"systems built to last, not patched together."*

## Audience pain points by ICP

### HVAC contractors
- Missing emergency calls because leads sit in a general inbox
- No way to prioritise urgent vs routine jobs
- Following up too slowly and losing to competitors
- Owner doing everything manually

### Real estate agents/brokers
- Leads from multiple sources (website, Zillow, referral) going to different places
- No system to score buyer readiness
- Slow follow-up on listing inquiries
- Admin time killing selling time

### Professional services / consulting firms
- Proposal process is manual and inconsistent
- Client onboarding has no documented workflow
- Invoice and project tracking done in spreadsheets
- No visibility into pipeline status
