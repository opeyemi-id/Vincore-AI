## Mission

To help service-based and project-based businesses recover lost revenue and operate with the efficiency of companies three times their size — by building intelligent automation systems that are structured, documented, and built to last.

---

## What makes Vincore different

1. **ISO/GRC background** — every system is designed with governance and risk discipline. Documented, structured, tested. Not patched together.
2. **Practitioner-led** — the founder has personally built the systems in the portfolio. No outsourcing, no theory.
3. **Business-first, tools-second** — we diagnose before we automate. We never recommend a tool before understanding the business.
4. **Done-for-you** — clients get working systems, not courses, templates, or advice.

## Services

### 1. Revenue Systems
Automated systems that ensure no lead goes cold and no opportunity is missed.

Includes: speed-to-lead automation, lead scoring, lead routing, CRM setup and automation, pipeline visibility dashboards, follow-up sequences.

### 2. Operations Systems
Automated workflows that replace manual, repetitive, error-prone processes.

Includes: invoice processing automation, ticket escalation systems, client onboarding workflows, internal reporting automation, task routing and handoff systems.

### 3. AI Advisory
Strategic guidance on how to deploy AI inside a service business responsibly and effectively.

Includes: AI readiness assessment, agent design and deployment, GTM agent setup, AI workflow mapping, staff AI adoption planning.
