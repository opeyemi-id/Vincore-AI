# Pricing and packaging

> Note: These are current-stage prices for Vincore AI's first clients (pre-social proof phase). Prices should increase as case studies and testimonials accumulate.

---

## Project-based pricing

### Starter System — $300–$500
**Best for:** Single-problem automation. One workflow, one use case.

Includes:
- Discovery call (30 mins)
- System design and build
- Testing and deployment
- Handover documentation
- 1 round of revisions

Examples: Basic speed-to-lead system, simple lead capture and notification, single Zapier workflow with Airtable base

---

### Growth System — $800–$1,500
**Best for:** Multi-step automation covering a core business function.

Includes:
- Discovery call (60 mins)
- Full system architecture document
- Build across 3–5 tools
- Testing against real scenarios
- Handover call and full documentation
- 2 rounds of revisions
- 2 weeks of post-launch support

Examples: Full speed-to-lead with scoring and routing, property management invoice + ticket system, real estate lead aggregation and CRM setup

---

### Full Operations Build — $2,000–$4,000
**Best for:** End-to-end automation of a business function (revenue or operations).

Includes:
- 2 discovery calls
- Full process mapping and audit
- Multi-system architecture
- Build across 5+ tools
- Staged testing and QA
- Staff walkthrough and training
- Full documentation suite
- 30-day post-launch support

Examples: Complete revenue system (capture → score → route → follow-up → CRM), full operations automation (onboarding → delivery → invoicing → reporting)

---

## Retainer pricing (future)

Once a client has a system in place, offer ongoing management:

- **Maintenance retainer:** $150–$300/month — monitoring, minor updates, Zapier task management
- **Growth retainer:** $400–$800/month — monthly system reviews, new automation builds, reporting

---

## Pricing principles

1. **Never lead with price.** Understand the problem first. Price based on the value of the problem solved, not the hours spent.
2. **Anchor to ROI.** A $500 speed-to-lead system that recovers one lost job per month pays for itself in the first week.
3. **Scope tightly.** Avoid scope creep by defining deliverables clearly in writing before starting.
4. **Deposits.** Charge 50% upfront for all projects over $300. Non-negotiable.
5. **Raise prices** after the first 2–3 clients provide testimonials or case study approval.
